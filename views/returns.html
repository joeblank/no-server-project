Returns & Exchanges

SALE ITEMS

  • Items marked as 'sale' are inded final. No returns or exchanges.

    • RETURNS

  • Full refunds will be given on merchandise returned in new, unworn condition up to 7 days.
  • After 7 days, but before 30 days we will issue a store credit.
  • Items are no longer eligible for return after 30 days of purchase and are considered final sale.
  • Refunds are credited to the original form of payment.
  • If a return is initiated within the 30-day return period. Email us at Contact@Articlemenswear.com, and we'll send you a prepaid return label.

    • EXCHANGES

  • Merchandise returned in new, unworn condition may be exchanged for store credit within 30 days of purchase.
  • Items are no longer eligible for return or exchange after 30 days of purchase and are considered final sale.
  • Any fees associated with shipping will not be refunded.
